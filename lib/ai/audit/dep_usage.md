Run: bash lib/ai/audit/run.sh
Current sites: 312

Every eta-ai call site for an allowed external dependency is listed here. The
catalog is not a gate; it is the truth-of-record.

Allowed production dependencies for eta-ai core:

- eta
- eta-redacted
- eta-stream
- eta-http
- yojson

Transport-neutral AI packages must not depend on `eta_http_eio`,
`eta_http_js`, Eio, or js_of_ocaml. Backend-specific AI transport packages are
audited separately.
