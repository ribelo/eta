## Provenance

- Case files are machine-generated by `gen.sh` (compiles against the main
  workspace `lib/eta` build) and are character-identical to the locked
  expect strings in `test/core_common/cause_render_common_suites.ml`.
- Cases 1–5 are the one-pager corpus minimum; case 6 is the ugly-composite
  case from the one-pager's problem statement.
- Interrupt ids in case 3/4 renders (`interrupt: 1`, `interrupt#2`) are
  fresh per generator run; identity, not the number, is the review signal.

## Notes for the board

- `pp_compact`'s contract: single line, total, newline-free; defect span
  names, annotations, and backtraces are omitted by design (see
  `lib/eta/cause.mli`). Question 7 asks you to judge that omission.
- The red-team (ugliest-cause attacks, including this corpus's case 6 shape
  nested deeper) is in `../redteam/VERDICT.md` — read it only after rating
  the corpus yourself, to avoid anchoring.
